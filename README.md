📊 Sales & Customers Dashboards | 2023
Interactive Tableau dashboards analyzing Sales & Customer performance for 2023, with YoY comparison and detailed insights.
________________________________________
🚀 Project Overview
This project includes two Tableau dashboards:
✅ Sales Dashboard
Provides a full overview of:
•	Total Sales: $733K (+20.4% YoY)
•	Total Profit: $93K (+14.2% YoY)
•	Total Quantity Sold: 12K (+26.8% YoY)
Dashboard features:
•	Monthly trend lines
•	Highest & lowest month indicators
•	Sales & profit by product subcategory
•	Sales & profit trends over time (avg lines included)

________________________________________
✅ Customers Dashboard
Shows customer-related KPIs:
•	Total Customers: 693 (+8.6% YoY)
•	Avg Sales per Customer: $1,058 (+10.8% YoY)
•	Total Orders: 1,687 (+28.29% YoY)
Dashboard features:
•	Monthly customer trends
•	Subcategory performance
•	Top 10 Customers by Profit (name, sales, orders, date of last order)
